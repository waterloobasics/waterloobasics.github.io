Get Involved
Joining clubs, teams, and societies within the university is a great way to get to meet new people, explore your interests, and have fun! This page was designed to help you discover the wide variety of ways you can get involved on campus.

Getting Involved in the Faculty of Engineering
Waterloo Engineering Society (EngSoc)
The Waterloo Engineering Society is like a student council that organizes events, manages an endowment fund, and provides a voice for students at Waterloo. They also provide a variety of support programs for students including resumé critiques, exam banks, workshops, and more!
EngSoc
Student Design Teams
Student design teams are groups you can join to work on large-scale projects and compete in inter-university competitions. Joining these teams is great for making connections and gaining hands-on experience.
Design Teams Directory
EngiQueers
EngiQueers is a drop-in, anonymous chapter of EngiQueers Canada, focused on connecting LGBTQ+ students in engineering and providing them with a place they can feel at home and accepted.
UW EngiQueers Facebook
Women in Engineering (WiE)
Women in Engineering (WiE) at the University of Waterloo exists to support current female engineers and university students, while encouraging the next generation of women to pursue careers in engineering. They provide programs, resources, and networking opportunities for women in engineering.
